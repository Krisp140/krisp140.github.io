Spaghetti Carbonara

Prep time: 10 min | Cook time: 15 min | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g guanciale or pancetta, cubed
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil salted water for pasta.
  2. Fry guanciale in a pan until crisp (medium heat).
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and cheese with plenty of black pepper.
  4. Cook pasta al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
  5. Toss hot pasta with guanciale fat, remove from heat. Quickly mix in egg mixture to create a creamy sauce. Add pasta water if needed. Serve immediately.
Chicken Tikka Masala

Prep time: 20 min | Cook time: 40 min | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala spice mix
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 can tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and half the spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill or sear chicken pieces until charred. Set aside.
  3. Saut√© onion, add remaining spices and tomato puree. Simmer for 10 mins.
  4. Add cream and chicken to the sauce. Simmer for another 10 mins until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep time: 15 min | Cook time: 10 min | Serves: 24

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar, 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract, 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Beat butter, sugars, and vanilla until creamy. Add eggs, one at a time.
  4. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop rounded tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheet. Bake 9 to 11 minutes until golden brown.
